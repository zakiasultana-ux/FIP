FAQ

Activity FAQs

What activities are available at the resort?

Our resort offers activities such as kayaking, hiking, fishing, spa treatments, and guided nature tours.

Do I need to book activities in advance?

While some activities can be booked on arrival, we recommend reserving in advance to guarantee availability.

Are the activities included in the package?

Some activities are included in our packages, while others may have an additional fee. Please check specific package details.

Are there activities available year-round?

Yes, we offer seasonal activities, including skiing and snowshoeing in the winter and water sports in the summer.
