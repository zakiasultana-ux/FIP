FAQ

Reservation FAQs

How can I make a reservation?

Reservations can be made online through our website or by calling our customer service team.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before check-in receive a full refund. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Can I modify my reservation?

Yes, modifications can be made up to 24 hours before check-in, subject to availability.

Do I need to pay in advance?

A deposit is required at the time of booking, with the remaining balance due upon arrival.
